1Click is an incremental game where you click to earn points and upgrade your clicking power. It was made by KKE (Baki you can't even smell my name). Here are some things you might want to know:
On PC, press J,N,W or up arrow to click.
Press 1, 2, 3, 4 to apply the 1st, 2nd, 3rd or 4th upgrade

Login and play, press save to save progress and load to load progress from cloud, similar to GEOMETRY DASH
Do not open MORE THAN 1 tab with the same account
Have fun
